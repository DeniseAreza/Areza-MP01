Welcome to CoffeeFave

This is where we will talk about my favorite drinks, my paintings that you can view, and some coffee recipes that you can try at home. So, now, here are my favorite drinks :)

Iced Coffee with Chocolate

Espresso shot with cold, creamy, and sweet milk. Added with chocolate syrup on the bottom and sides of the drink.

"I like it very much because of the combination of sweetness of the chocolate and the coffee. "

Creamy Coffee Float

Espresso shot with ice cream on top. Added with chocolate syrup on top of the drink.

"I like it very much because of the creaminess and sweetness of the ice cream on top. It is like a dessert for me."
